Human-Centered AI Metacognitive Learning Model (HAIML)

HAIML is a framework developed by Catheryn Reardon, PhD to guide how students learn with AI while maintaining critical thinking, human oversight, and intentional engagement.

Rather than treating AI as a replacement for learning, HAIML emphasizes structured interaction, reflection on how AI shapes thinking, and informed ethical decision-making.
