About

I am an Assistant Teaching Professor of Psychology at Arizona State University. My work focuses on human-centered AI, online learning, and ethical integration of emerging technologies in education.

Research

My research explores instructor social presence, human-AI interaction, and the role of generative AI in enhancing student engagement, critical thinking, and ethical decision-making.

HAIML Framework

The Human-Centered AI Metacognitive Learning Model (HAIML) emphasizes learning through experience, reflection, and ethical evaluation of AI use.
