Featured Framework

Human-Centered AI Metacognitive Learning Model (HAIML)

A framework for integrating AI into learning in ways that preserve human agency, support metacognitive reflection, and promote ethical decision-making.

Developed by Catheryn Reardon, PhD

01

Human Agency

Structured AI interaction that keeps the learner in control of thinking, judgment, and outcomes.

02

Metacognitive Reflection

Deliberate reflection on how AI shapes reasoning, confidence, and the learning process.

03

Ethical Decision-Making

Informed, transparent, and critical engagement with AI in educational contexts.

Actively implemented in online psychology courses at Arizona State University.

Featured Resource

Difficult Conversations AI Practice Tool

An interactive, human-centered AI role-play experience designed to support communication, reflection, empathy, and ethical decision-making.

This tool helps educators, students, and professionals practice navigating complex interpersonal situations through AI-mediated role-play. It creates a structured space for feedback, reflection, and skill-building while preserving the central role of human judgment.
