Human-Centered AI Metacognitive Learning Model (HAIML)

The Human-Centered AI Metacognitive Learning Model (HAIML) is a framework designed to guide how students learn with AI while maintaining human agency, critical thinking, and ethical responsibility. Rather than positioning AI as a replacement for learning, HAIML emphasizes structured engagement, reflection on how AI shapes thinking, and intentional decision-making.
