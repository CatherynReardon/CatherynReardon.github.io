Human Expertise in the Age of Agentic AI

As AI becomes more autonomous, human expertise becomes more important, not less.

This framework illustrates the evolving relationship between artificial intelligence and human expertise. As AI progresses from tools to copilots and increasingly autonomous agents, the human role shifts from information delivery toward judgment, reflection, ethical reasoning, metacognition, and accountability.
