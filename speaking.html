Speaking Topics

I speak on topics related to human-centered AI in education, metacognition, ethical AI integration, instructor presence, online learning, and AI-supported teaching practices. My presentations are designed to be practical, reflective, and grounded in real educational contexts.

Workshop Areas

  • Human-centered AI in course design
  • Structured AI use guidelines for students
  • Metacognitive reflection and ethical AI use
  • Instructor presence in online learning
  • Human-in-the-loop AI grading and feedback

Audience

My workshops and presentations are relevant to faculty, instructional designers, academic leaders, and organizations interested in thoughtful AI integration. I especially enjoy helping educators move from uncertainty about AI to more intentional, ethical, and pedagogically grounded practice.

Approach

My approach to speaking and faculty development is collaborative and accessible. I focus on practical examples, reflective questions, and models that participants can adapt to their own classrooms, programs, and institutional contexts.
